About Me

I consider myself an experienced IT professional with a broad knowledge of hardware, software, and networking technologies. I'm proficient in database design and development, software design, programming and debugging. I'm also experienced in teaching classes and training a variety of people in various settings. I've also aquired quite a bit of Data Science experience extracting and analyzing data, turning it into useful information and then visualizing it.

My daily duties in my current and previous positions include designing, building and maintaining websites, overseeing day-to-day maintenance of existing sites, along with testing and implementing fixes for these sites. All current sites I deal with now also must be accessible according to current W3C standards. The bulk of my work for the last 7+ years has been utilizing WordPress, PHP, HTML, CSS and Javascript. Data Science work includes several years utilizing Python and R. I'm currently working in and converting websites to a Django/Wagtail framework.

My hobbies include gaming, some light game development, watching Sci-fi and Dystopian movies and also watching Pittsburgh sports.
